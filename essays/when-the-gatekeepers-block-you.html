When the gatekeepers block you

August 2, 2019

If you feel like you’re getting blocked from what you want by some unfair system (group of people) that doesn’t want you, you can….

  • Fight even harder to be accepted by them
  • Settle for the next best thing
  • Change what you want
  • Complain loudly enough about the system to try to change their minds
  • Decide to go after what you really wanted in the first place, whether or not they want to go there with you
  • Make your own system, one that you believe in

Which sound most appealing?
